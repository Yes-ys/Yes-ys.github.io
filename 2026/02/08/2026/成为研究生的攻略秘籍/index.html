英语自我介绍

英文口语回答（精简、自然、适配面试、可直接背诵）

Talking about my strengths, firstly, I have a solid theoretical foundation and strong interest in artificial intelligence. I have systematically learned core knowledge including machine learning, reinforcement learning and multi-task learning, which lays a good foundation for my future research.

Secondly, I am good at practical operation. I keep up with the latest AI tools and have hands-on experience with mainstream tools such as CodeX and Claude Code. I prefer to combine theoretical knowledge with practical practice.

Thirdly, I am diligent and experienced in scientific research. I have joined the research group since the first semester of my sophomore year. With two consecutive years of research accumulation, I have formed good academic thinking and research habits.

As for my weakness, I sometimes focus too much on implementing technical details and ignore sorting out the overall framework of the research. Now I am used to summarizing research ideas regularly to improve my overall logical thinking ability.

中文对照（方便理解背诵）

我的优点主要有三点。第一，我对人工智能方向抱有浓厚兴趣，并且具备扎实的理论基础，系统学习过机器学习、强化学习、多任务学习等核心内容，为后续科研打下了基础。

第二，我擅长动手实践。我会主动跟进前沿AI技术，熟练使用CodeX、Claude Code等主流工具，习惯将理论知识落地到实践中。

第三，我做事勤奋、科研积累充足。我从大二上就进入课题组，连续两年深耕科研，养成了良好的科研思维和学术习惯。

我的缺点是，我有时会过于专注技术细节的实现，忽略整体研究框架的梳理。目前我会定期总结科研思路，刻意锻炼自己的整体逻辑思维。
